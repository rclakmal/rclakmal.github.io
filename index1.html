Let me briefly explain about the study

The study contain two steps. First I present you a small online shopping challenge. Later, I would request you to fill out a quick survey on your shopping experience. That's it! Not that hard! And it would not take more than 5-7 mins. I promise! ☺

Let's Checkout the Challenge

Here is the challenge

Imagine one out of these two situations.

  • Your best friend is graduating soon. You plan to make their day special by a nice graduation gift(s)
  • One of your family member has their birthday next week. You plan to shop for a nice birthday gift(s)
Did you choose one ? Nice!
You have a budget of 1500$ to buy these gift(s) which you get through a discount coupon. The challenge is to make the maximum use out of this discount coupan.
