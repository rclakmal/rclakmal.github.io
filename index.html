ආයුබෝවන්.

I'm Lakmal Padmakumara.

I'm a programmer, an engineer and a science enthusiastic from Sri Lanka, currently based in Munich, Germany. I graduated from my Masters in Computer Science in 2017 from Technical University of Munich. I also completed my Honors degree in Technology Management at CDTM Munich. My primary education took place at President's College, Homagama and my secondary education at Royal College, Colombo. I graduated from University of Moratuwa in 2012 with my BSc in Computer Science & Engineering. I'm interested in exploring topics like product management in agile environment, UX/CX, design thinking/customer value co-creation. Read my full CV

Oh btw, if you were wondering about those strange but beautiful characters on top of my name, ආයුබෝවන්, that's how you say 'Welcome' in my mother toungue, Sinhalese. Actually, it's not just a simple welcome rather a wish for a long & healthy life.
