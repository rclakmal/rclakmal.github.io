I'm Lakmal Padmakumara.

I'm a programmer, an engineer and a science enthusiastic from Sri Lanka, currently based in Munich, Germany following my Masters in Computer Science at Technical University of Munich. I'm also following my Honors degree in Technology Management at CDTM Munich. I completed my primary education at President's College, Homagama and my secondary education at Royal College, Colombo. I graduated from University of Moratuwa in 2012 with my BSc in Computer Science & Engineering. I'm interested in learning more about areas like agile software engineering practices, machine learning, data mining & user experience. My favourite programming languages so far are Python, Java and Javascript (in that order). In year 2017, I hope to learn & practice more about technologies including, but not limited, to Apache Spark, MongoDB, NoSQL, AngularJS and NodeJs. Read my full resume

Oh btw, if you were wondering about those strange but beautiful characters on top of my name, ආයුබෝවන්, that's how you say 'Welcome' in my mother toungue, Sinhalese. Actually, it's not just a simple welcome rather a wish for a long & healthy life.
